College of Engineering Chengannur [CEC]

Mission
To impart engineering education that enables the students to design, develop and maintain systems, products and process for the society utilizing the evolving technology.

Vision
To emerge as an institution of excellence that imparts knowledge through education, learning, and research for the inclusive development of society.

About Us
The College of Engineering Chengannur commonly known as CEC, is an engineering institute in the state of Kerala, India that was established by the Government of Kerala under the aegis of the Institute of Human Resources Development in 1993. The college is located in Chengannur, Alappuzha
